Data types

Overview
The wonderful thing about TV Database is that it downloads all the available information about a series automatically - the only thing you have to input is the name. The following list and the subpages corresponding to each of the items are intended to familiarize you with what information you're able to obtain.

 Major data types
  • Languages - the languages spoken throughout the series
  • Networks - the networks on which the series are broadcasted
  • Genres - the stylistic cinematographical genres the series belong to
  • People and acting roles - the actors that play various roles in different series
  • Series and episodes - the series themselves and the information they contain
For more information on how this data is obtained during update, see the Updater section.
