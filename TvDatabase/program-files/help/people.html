People and acting roles

Overview
These are what makes loyal TV watchers excited, the actors and their acting.

It is important to note that in the application, as in real life, actors (people) may play one or more roles (here called acting roles), usually across several series. Therefore, we will treat them separately.

 People
People (person, in singular) are the actual people employed to play an acting role. In the app, they serve as the base on which the roles are built and displayed.

 To learn more about the Person data type, see the Person section.

 Acting roles
Acting roles are the personae played by actors in a given TV series. They rely on the Person data type and have, among others, images associated with them.

 To learn more about the Acting role data type, see the Acting role section.

 You may also go back to the data types section.
