Languages

Overview
These are the languages spoken throughout the series by the actors (and possibly through means of non-verbal communication). They exist in order to inform potential viewers of whether they would understand the dialogues without subtitles.

In the application, the languages are indexed in a list in the database, in order to avoid repetitions, but otherwise have limited practical use.

 Unlike other data types, which are added to the database only when necessary, languages are downloaded at the beginning of the update in bulk, and kept during all times. This however, because of their small number, does not affect performance in any way.

 In-app attributes
  • id - a unique text identifier for the language, which is obtained on update from thetvdb.com (not generated locally). Used for safe reference.
  • name - the name of the language, which is displayed in-app.
For more information on how this data is obtained during update, see the Updater section.
